This article is about the BMW subsidiary established in 1998. It is not to be confused with Rolls-Royce Motors. For other uses, see Rolls-Royce.

Although the Rolls-Royce brand has been in use since 1906, the Rolls-Royce Motor Cars subsidiary of BMW AG has no direct relationship to Rolls-Royce-branded vehicles produced prior to 2003 other than being a major engine and other supplier prior to 2003.The Rolls-Royce Phantom four-door sedan was the first product offered for sale in 2003. Mercedes-Benz

This article is about the vehicle manufacturer. For other uses, see Mercedes-Benz (disambiguation).

Mercedes-Benz commonly referred to as just Mercedes, is a German luxury automotive marque. Both Mercedes-Benz and Mercedes-Benz AG (a Mercedes-Benz Group subsidiary established in 2019) are headquartered in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg, Germany.Mercedes-Benz produces consumer luxury vehicles and commercial vehicles. Its first Mercedes-Benz-badged vehicles were produced in 1926. In 2018, Mercedes-Benz was the largest seller of premium vehicles in the world, having sold 2.31 million passenger cars.
